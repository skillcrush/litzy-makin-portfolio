Hi, I'm Litzy Makin!

I'm a front end developer specializing in creating responsive, fast websites for clients with a strong background in marketing.

Front End Developer & Marketer

For many years I was immersed in the marketing world. I started as a copywriter, then moved to a marketing strategist. Eventually, I worked my way to becoming a marketing manager, focusing on increasing engagement rates and launching products.

After a career break to focus on caring for my aging parents, I decided I needed a career change. I loved all things internet and was always curious about how to build websites. I enrolled in a front end developer course and started learning HTML, CSS, responsive design, and JavaScript. While working on my first HTML and CSS class, I knew this was what I was meant to do! I was also excited because I could see myself using my marketing background to promote clients' businesses and increase their customer base.

When I'm not developing, I like spending time at the beach and watching documentaries. My home base in Belize has some of the best beaches in the world, so the beach has always been my happy place. My favorite documentaries are on sports or true crime.
